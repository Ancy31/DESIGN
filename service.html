How we works

6 steps to interior design

Client Meeting

Scheduling a client meeting is a vital part of the initial collaboration in finding out what clients truly expect based on their needs, taste, and style. This helps in understanding the client’s vision and aspirations.

Innovative Design

Design is all about creativity. D2D’s expertise enables creating designs that meet a client's vision - within the budget

Creative Visualization

WD2D utilizes 3D rendering software to visualize a project enabling clients to understand how it will look. This further helps in making modifications besides expediting the decision-making process saving a lot of time in project completion.

Perfect Execution

With careful attention to space, style and function D2D crafts interiors that are unique and reflective of the client’s aspirations besides striving to explore creative opportunities within each project undertaken.

On-Site Supervision

We offers professional on-site supervision to ensure that the project is executed with a high level of quality and matches all agreed plans, drawings and designs. Also clients are updated with the progress on a regular basis.

Quality

electing the right materials is accorded priority and care is taken to select quality materials used. The emphasis is on meeting a wide range of strict quality regulations and standard besides focussing on excellence in outcomes.
